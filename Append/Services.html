Executive Search and Recruitment

Hiring the best fit talent is what gives an organization the right foundation & wings to scale culture and strategy. Our researchers and associates have expertise at identifying and acquiring the right talent your company needs & deserves and have serviced wide range of clients, from early stage Start-up’s to large multinational companies. Regardless of the position we are recruiting for, we place equal balance among a candidate’s talent, experience and interpersonal fit. Every hire is a valuable component of your organization’s philosophy. Therefore, our search and recruiting process involves a great deal of upfront observation and understanding of not only the products, brand identity and short term/long term goals, but also the people, personalities, physical environment and overall company culture. This allows us to gain a deep understanding of the dynamics and nuances for each role. If required and asked for by the client, we also help in conducting psychometric assessments, closing offer negotiation and undertaking background verification checks.

Building Blocks

Getting HR Policies and People Practices right from the very beginning and building a firm foundation is not only critical for compliance and attracting top talent, but also essential for creating a positive workplace culture that fosters productivity and performance. However, investing time on these for a start-up or a rapidly growing company is always a challenge. That’s precisely where we come with our expertise to help your business have a firm foundation on which to grow. Our approach is scalable and adaptable and we understand that all start-ups / evolving organizations have varying HR needs - need effective yet affordable HR support as a one-time effort to define and compile your HR Policies & Strategy OR an HR Partner for a few hours per week / month OR an HR professional embedded in your team. As you grow, we either take the journey along with you or help you fully transition to an in-house HR team……the choice is off-course, yours.

Retained Advisory Services and Custom People Solutions

No two business’ challenges and goals are the same, so your people strategy shouldn’t be either. At The BlueRibbon Partners, we work with you to understand the unique needs of your workplace & workforce and build the right HR processes to help accomplish immediate needs and long-term goals. We have the experience and expertise to be your partners in defining, designing and implementing Talent Strategy, Performance Management Process, Employee Experience & Engagement Plans and Strategic alignment of People Processes & Business Vision. If needed, we also offer Executive Advisory services, with Exec Advisor being a coach, mentor and supportive guide, embedded into your leadership team. This gives you immediate access to years of expertise of the attached advisor, supporting review and evolution of systems and procedures and add value at the leadership table by contributing to business-critical decisions (back-end support from rest of the team is only an added bonus!).

We look forward to the opportunity of connecting with you and understand how we can partner through our comprehensive HR Consulting Services
